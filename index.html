Introduction

Welcome to my Interaction Design project. I am going to talk about gamifying learning apps. Our aim is to make learning more fun for people to keep them engaged and draw more people into the app.
Gamification is the act of applying game design principles to non-games, such as for education. The purpose of this is to make the application more engaging and enjoyable to interact with.
This project will involve multiple sections, including a competitive analysis, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing.
The competitive audit will allow me to see strengths and weaknesses of existing educational apps and find out what I should and shouldn't do.
Wireframing and prototypinh will let me design and evelop the interface of the app and see how a user would interact with it.
Usability testing will then be conducted to gather feedback from people who have used the app and this feedback will be used to refind the app and make it better.
Overall my goal is to create a learning app that will not only educates users but be fun to use.
